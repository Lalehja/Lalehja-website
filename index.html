About Us

La-Lehja Logistics and Delivery Services is a registered franchise of BlueDart and DHL Courier Services in Pithampur for Domestic and International freight. We provide a single window for all your logistics requirements through our in-house facilities, and our association with renowned networks helps us to cater worldwide. We have an expert team with over 35 years of combined experience from Bluedart in the logistics industry, especially in critical documents, heavy lifts, and hazardous cargo. We have our own inventory of special containers to serve ODC trade.
